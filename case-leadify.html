Case Study: Sales Automation

Building Leadify CRM For Business Scale

Automating sales funnels for high-growth service teams in Delhi NCR and Patna.

The Problem

Leadify was growing but their sales team was drowning in manual data entry using Google Sheets. Leads would go 24-48 hours without a follow-up, resulting in a 40% lead drop-off rate as customers moved to faster competitors.

They needed a central dashboard where every lead could be tracked from discovery to final payment automatically.

The Solution

TechSolutions built a custom CRM with automated WhatsApp and Email triggers. When a new lead arrives via their website or Meta Ads, an automated introductory message is sent instantly. The sales team receives a dashboard notification and tasks are prioritized based on lead intent.

Technologies used: Node.js, React, WhatsApp API, MongoDB.

The Result

Lead response time dropped from 24+ hours to just 5 minutes on average. Sales revenue saw an immediate 35% jump within the first 60 days of implementation. The administrative team saved over 15 hours every week on reporting and manual follow-up scheduling.
