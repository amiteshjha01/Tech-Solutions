Leadify CRM: From Manual Spreadsheets to Automated Sales Pipeline

How TechSolutions built a custom CRM on its proprietary platform to eliminate manual follow-ups, automate lead communication, and help a growing sales team close more deals faster.

The Challenge

Leadify was scaling its sales operations but their team was managing all leads manually through Google Sheets. Leads were going 24 to 48 hours without a follow-up, resulting in a 40% lead drop-off rate as prospects moved to competitors who responded faster.

They needed a centralised CRM where every lead — from initial contact through to final payment — could be tracked, assigned, and followed up automatically, without relying on manual effort from the sales team.

The Solution

TechSolutions configured and built a customised CRM on its proprietary platform, with automated WhatsApp and email triggers integrated into the lead capture workflow. When a new lead arrives — via the website or Meta Ads — an introductory message is sent instantly, and the sales team receives a dashboard notification with the lead prioritised by intent score.

The entire pipeline — from lead capture to deal closure — is visible in one interface, with automated reminders replacing manual scheduling entirely.

Technologies used: Node.js, React, WhatsApp Business API, MongoDB.

The Outcome

Lead response time dropped from 24+ hours to an average of 5 minutes. Within the first 60 days of going live, sales revenue increased by 35% — a direct result of faster follow-ups and better pipeline visibility. The administrative team reclaimed over 15 hours per week previously spent on manual reporting and follow-up scheduling.
