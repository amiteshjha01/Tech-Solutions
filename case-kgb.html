KGB Hotels: Custom CRM & Booking System Across 3 Properties

How TechSolutions built a unified digital and operations infrastructure for a growing hotel group in Visakhapatnam — replacing manual processes with a custom CRM, WhatsApp booking integration, and a centralized management interface.

The Challenge

KGB Hotels operates three distinct properties across Visakhapatnam — near RTC Complex, the Railway Station, and Ram Nagar — each serving a different segment of guests. Without a unified system, managing bookings, guest communication, and operations across all three locations was largely manual and inefficient.

They needed a technology solution that could unify all three properties under one platform, support 24-hour check-ins, and give guests a direct, frictionless booking experience — without relying on third-party aggregators.

The Solution

TechSolutions designed and built a custom CRM and booking ecosystem that unified all three KGB properties on a single platform. Each property retained its own identity and positioning:

  • ✦ KGB Coolriver: Corporate and premium accommodation near KA Paul Convention Hall — targeted at business travelers.
  • ✦ KGB Legend Grand: Quality stays at accessible rates for budget-conscious and mid-range guests.
  • ✦ KGB Villa Homes: A comfortable, home-like retreat for families and leisure travelers exploring Vizag.

We integrated WhatsApp Booking directly into the platform to reduce friction and enable instant guest communication — aligning with how the hotel's customers actually prefer to book.

The Outcome

"TechSolutions built a CRM that actually fits how we work. It simplified our operations across 3 properties and saved us hours of manual effort every single day."

With a unified CRM, automated guest communication, and a mobile-first booking system, KGB Hotels significantly reduced manual workload across all three properties. The platform now handles check-in coordination, booking tracking, and guest follow-ups — freeing the team to focus on delivering a better guest experience.
