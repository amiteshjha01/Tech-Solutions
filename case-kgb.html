The Problem

KGB Hotels needed a unified digital presence for their expanding collection in Visakhapatnam. With properties near RTC Complex, the Railway Station, and Ram Nagar, the challenge was to communicate the unique value of each location while maintaining a single, premium brand identity.

They required a system that handled 24-hour check-ins and offered a direct, "no-hassle" booking experience for corporate, family, and leisure travelers.

The Solution

TechSolutions architected a multi-property digital ecosystem. We highlighted three distinct experiences:

  • ✦ KGB Coolriver: Corporate & Premium stay near KA Paul Convention Hall.
  • ✦ KGB Legend Grand: Quality stays at accessible rates for budget-conscious guests.
  • ✦ KGB Villa Homes: A warm, home-like retreat for families exploring Vizag.

We integrated instant WhatsApp Booking to bypass complex forms, aligning with their "genuine care" philosophy.

The Impact

"To make every guest feel genuinely at home — through comfort, affordability, and service that goes beyond expectation."

The new platform re-established KGB Hotels as the definitive choice in Vizag. With 24/7 technical support and a mobile-first booking funnel, property occupancy increased across all segments.
