The Mission

Located in the heart of Tirupati, Sri Ganesh Residency is committed to providing comfortable, clean, and welcoming accommodations. They needed to digitize their diverse room inventory—ranging from Deluxe Rooms to large Family Rooms for 10 and 16 guests.

Their challenge was to communicate their core values—Excellence, Integrity, and Care—to a digital audience while competing with large aggregators.

The Solution

TechSolutions built a custom booking portal designed for family gatherings and religious tourism. We focused on the "Sri Ganesh Residency Advantage":

  • ✦ Diverse Inventory: Easy selection between Deluxe, Double Deluxe, and massive 16-guest rooms.
  • ✦ Trust Signals: Transparency in pricing and 24/7 attentive care.
  • ✦ Localized Reach: SEO optimization targeting spiritual travelers visiting Andhra Pradesh.

The Impact

By providing a peaceful retreat with modern amenities and a personal touch, Sri Ganesh Residency has seen a 200% increase in direct inquiries. The simplified digital booking process for large family groups has made it a favorite for regional travelers.
