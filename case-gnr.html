The Challenge

Located in the heart of Tirupati, Sri Ganesh Residency had a strong reputation for comfortable, clean, and welcoming accommodation for family groups and religious travelers. However, the property had no website, no online booking capability, and no digital presence to speak of.

Their room inventory — ranging from Deluxe Rooms to large Family Rooms accommodating up to 16 guests — was managed entirely offline, making it difficult to reach travelers planning visits to Andhra Pradesh from across the country.

The Solution

TechSolutions designed and developed a custom booking portal and website tailored specifically to the property's target audience — family groups and spiritual travelers. Our scope of work included:

  • ✦ Custom Booking Portal: Easy room selection across Deluxe, Double Deluxe, and large group configurations of up to 16 guests.
  • ✦ Trust & Transparency: Clear pricing, property details, and 24/7 support messaging built directly into the site experience.
  • ✦ Local SEO Strategy: Targeted optimization for spiritual and family travel search terms across Andhra Pradesh and Tirupati.

The Outcome

Within 90 days of launch, Sri Ganesh Residency recorded a 200% increase in direct inquiries — reducing dependence on third-party aggregators and improving booking margins. The property's digital presence now accurately reflects the quality and warmth of the experience it delivers to guests.

"Genuine hospitality, now discoverable."
