Our Story

Custom Software & Technology Built for Indian Businesses

At TechSolutions, we build the software that runs your business. From our own customizable CRM to full-scale web apps and LMS platforms, we give Indian SMBs access to enterprise-grade technology — without the enterprise price tag.

Software That Solves Real Business Problems

Founded in 2023, TechSolutions was built on a clear observation: most small and mid-sized businesses in India have strong products and services, but lack the right technology infrastructure to operate efficiently and scale.

We are a services-based technology company. Our core offering is our own customizable CRM platform, alongside custom websites, web applications, learning management systems (LMS), software testing, and digital marketing — all designed and delivered in-house.
